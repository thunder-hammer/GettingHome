# Getting Home

A text-based city survival game for mobile and desktop browsers.

## About

It's midnight. The bar closed early, your phone is almost dead, and there are no rides available. Twelve blocks stand between you and the safety of your apartment.

Play as a woman navigating the city streets late at night, making choices that test your **Courage**, **Street-Smarts**, and **Stamina**. Every block brings a new encounter — some dangerous, some surprising, some kind. Your decisions shape the journey and determine how you get home.

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. Enter your character's name (or keep the default)
3. Read each encounter and choose your response
4. Manage your three stats:
   - **Courage** — Helps you face threats head-on
   - **Street-Smarts** — Helps you think and talk your way through situations
   - **Stamina** — Your physical energy; if it hits zero, you can't go on
5. Survive twelve blocks to reach home

## Features

- **18 unique encounters** with multiple choices each
- **Stat-based outcomes** — your choices and stats determine success or failure
- **Multiple endings** based on your final stats and key decisions
- **Atmospheric dark theme** with typewriter text effects
- **Responsive design** — works on phones, tablets, and desktops
- **Auto-save** — close the tab and resume where you left off

## Running Locally

Simply open `index.html` in a browser, or use any local server:

```bash
# Python
python3 -m http.server 8000

# Node.js
npx serve .
```

Then visit `http://localhost:8000`.

## Tech Stack

Pure vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no build tools, no dependencies.
